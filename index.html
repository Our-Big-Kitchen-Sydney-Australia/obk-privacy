Privacy Policy

Our Big Kitchen — Volunteer Application

Last updated: 14 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Our Big Kitchen ("OBK", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use the OBK Volunteer mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to handling your information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Principles set out in the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).

By registering for and using the App, you agree to the collection and use of your information as described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information when you register and check in as a volunteer:

CategoryDetailsWhy we collect it
IdentityFirst name, last name, date of birthTo create and verify your volunteer record
ContactEmail address, phone numberTo communicate with you about volunteering and check-ins
OrganisationCompany name (corporate volunteers only)To group volunteers attending as part of a corporate team
ComplianceWorking With Children Check (WWCC) number and expiry dateTo meet our legal and child-safety obligations
Emergency contactName and email of a nominated contact (optional)To reach someone on your behalf in an emergency
Check-in activityDate and time of check-in via QR codeTo record volunteer attendance

2. Camera Access

The App requests access to your device camera solely to scan QR codes for checking in to a volunteer session. We do not take, store, or transmit photographs or video. Camera access is only active while you are on the scan screen, and you can decline or revoke this permission in your device settings at any time (the App remains usable for registration without it).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To register you as a volunteer and manage your participation;
  • To verify eligibility and child-safety compliance (WWCC);
  • To record attendance when you check in;
  • To contact you about your volunteering or in an emergency;
  • To meet our legal, safety, and record-keeping obligations.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

4. Storage and Third Parties

Your information is securely stored in our volunteer management system, which is hosted on Salesforce, a third-party customer relationship management platform that processes data on our behalf under its own security and privacy commitments. The App also stores your email address locally on your device so it can recognise you on return visits; you can clear this by removing the App or clearing its data.

5. Data Retention

We keep your personal information for as long as you remain an active volunteer and for as long afterwards as required to meet our legal, safety, and record-keeping obligations. When information is no longer needed, we take reasonable steps to delete or de-identify it.

6. Your Rights

You may, at any time:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you;
  • Ask us to correct information that is inaccurate or out of date;
  • Request that we delete your information, subject to our legal obligations;
  • Withdraw consent and stop using the App.

To make a request, contact us using the details below.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for use by adult volunteers and is not directed at children. We collect WWCC details for child-safety compliance, not from children themselves.

8. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against loss, misuse, and unauthorised access. Data is transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS) connections. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version will always be available at this page, with the "Last updated" date revised accordingly.
