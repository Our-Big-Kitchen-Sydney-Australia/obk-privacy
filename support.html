Our Big Kitchen — Volunteer Application

Welcome to support for the Our Big Kitchen (OBK) Volunteer app. The app lets volunteers register, look up their existing record, and check in to volunteer sessions by scanning a QR code. If you need help, we're here for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register as a volunteer?

Open the app and tap Register. Enter your details (name, email, phone, date of birth and, where required, your Working With Children Check number). Submit the form to create your volunteer record.

I've volunteered before — how do I find my record?

On the home screen, enter the email address you registered with to look up your existing volunteer profile.

How do I check in to a session?

Tap Check in and scan the QR code displayed at the venue using your device camera. The camera is used only to read the QR code.

The app won't let me use the camera.

Camera access is needed only for QR check-in. Enable it in your device Settings → Our Big Kitchen → Camera. You can still register and look up your record without camera access.

How do I update or delete my information?

Email us at the address above and we'll help you access, correct, or delete your personal information. See our Privacy Policy for more detail on how we handle your data.
